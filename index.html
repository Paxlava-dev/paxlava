Frequently asked questions

When do I get paid?
Funds release from escrow within 24h after buyer confirms delivery. Payouts processed weekly via Stripe.
What currencies are supported?
USD, EUR, AZN, RUB, GBP. Buyers pay in their currency; you receive your chosen payout currency.
Is there a listing fee?
No. Listing is completely free. We only take 10% when a sale is made.
What happens if there's a dispute?
Our team reviews the full conversation history and project delivery evidence. Funds stay in escrow until resolved.
Can I offer custom projects?
Yes. Buyers can message you about custom work. All negotiations happen on-platform.
How do I reduce the fee?
High-volume sellers (>$10k/month) can apply for our Enterprise plan. Contact us through the About page.

Our story

We're building
the IT bazaar
of the internet.

Paxlava was born in Baku, Azerbaijan — a country with a deep tradition of craftsmanship and commerce. We believe developers deserve a marketplace as good as their code.

Why Paxlava?

Most marketplaces are built for digital content — ebooks, music, courses. But developers are building full products: websites, apps, APIs, and SaaS platforms. These deserve a dedicated marketplace.

Paxlava is that marketplace. We connect developers who have built great things with buyers who need them — ready to deploy, right now.

The name comes from paxlava (baklava) — Azerbaijan's most beloved pastry, made from carefully layered ingredients, just like great software.

Our mission

To make it possible for any developer, anywhere in the world, to earn from the software they build — without needing investors, a sales team, or a marketing budget.

We believe great IT projects shouldn't sit in GitHub repos gathering dust. They should be discovered, bought, deployed, and used.
